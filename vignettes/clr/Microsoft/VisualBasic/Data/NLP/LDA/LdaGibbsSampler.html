LdaGibbsSampler

Description

Gibbs sampler for estimating the best assignments of topics for words and documents in a corpus. The algorithm is introduced in Tom Griffiths' paper "Gibbs sampling in the generative model of Latent Dirichlet Allocation" (2002). Gibbs sampler采样算法的实现 @author heinrich https://github.com/hankcs/LDA4j
